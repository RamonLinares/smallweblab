How the lab works

Build fast, publish live, let reality edit the idea.

AI speeds up the production loop, but the useful signal comes after launch: search behavior, play patterns, repeat visits, and whether the product earns another iteration.

  1. 01

    Prototype fast

    Start with the smallest form that can be published credibly.

  2. 02

    Ship in public

    Real pages, real links, real users, and measurable behavior.

  3. 03

    Keep only what earns attention

    Refine the projects that prove they deserve a longer life.
